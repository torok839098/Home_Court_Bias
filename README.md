## What Is Home Court Advantage?
Home court bias (also called home field advantage or home advantage) is the phenomenon where the home team is more likely to win a game than the visiting team.
## Purpose/Objective
To analyze how home court advantage has evolved in the NBA from 1953–2023, identify which teams and cities benefit most, and explore the psychological and business factors influencing this phenomenon.

## Hypothesis
If there is a home court advantage, then home teams will benefit significantly due to fan loyalty and familiarity of the location.

If not there is no home court advantage or bias, then this could be due to pressure of being in home and exceeding expectations.
